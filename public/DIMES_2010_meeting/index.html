DIMES 2010 Investigator Meeting


Information about the meeting (as of 10 August 2010):

Draft Agenda:  Posted here. The preliminary schedule likely omits some groups who may have preliminary results to share, and not all speakers are aware that they are on the docket. This is a good time, if you'd like to suggest shifts to the schedule.

Travel Arrangements:  If you have not already done so, now is also a great time to make travel arrangements.  DIMES meeting attendees are welcome to take advantage of the block of hotel rooms that is being held at both the Millennium and the Marriott for the CLIVAR workshop and WGOMD:
http://www.joss.ucar.edu/events/2010/clivar_wgomd_gsop/index.html#gsop
Other hotels are listed here:
 http://www.boulderlodging.com/map.asp

Head Count:  At the moment we don't think that we need a formal registration for the DIMES meeting, but once you know that you're attending (or how many people in your group are attending), please e-mail Kevin Speer <kspeer@fsu.edu> so that he can track numbers.

Decadal Predictability Workshop:  If you are planning to attend the "CLIVAR/WGOMD/GSOP Workshop on Decadal Variability, Predictability and Prediction: Understanding the Role of the Ocean" that precedes our meeting, please be sure to register.

DIMES Meeting Organization:  The DIMES meeting is structured with progress reports Friday morning, a joint session with the Working Group on Ocean Model Development Friday afternoon, discussion of upcoming UK cruises Saturday morning, and break-out sessions to address specific project planning issues Saturday afternoon.  The agenda includes some potential discussion questions, but almost certainly omits important topics, so please share your thoughts so we can get things added in.

Posters:  At lunchtime on Friday we will have some time to view posters.  This is a good opportunity to share preliminary results or simply to recycle a poster from another meeting.  Let us know if you have a poster to display, so that we can request an appropriate number of boards and make up a list of poster titles.
